Learning management system


Wipro Foundation's learning management system provides different courses on arts, languages, social science, and various other subjects through the partner's forum. Various other NGO platforms also offer various self-paced courses through the Partners Forum.
Students, teachers, and educators can avail the courses for their professional progress. You can access all the courses without any cost. Our LMS also host forums and webinars on a different subject. We are having a community discussion on education, health, ecology, and disaster response.
You can participate in community discussions through groups and forums.
